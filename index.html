ABOUT JG FIT COACH

Joel Gustin

I began my journey in the kitchen at age 15, not classically trained, but shaped by mentors who taught me the art of leadership, precision, and resilience. From lead cook to executive chef, I climbed the culinary ladder through grit and adaptability—earning every promotion through performance, not pedigree.

Growing up overweight, I discovered fitness as a transformative tool—not just for physical change, but for mental clarity and self-belief. Each goal I achieved in the gym taught me what hard work and success truly feel like. That drive carried me beyond the weight room into MMA, recreational soccer, half marathons, sprint triathlons, and ultimately, bodybuilding.

Though I've only recently stepped on stage, I've lived the bodybuilding lifestyle for years—training, cooking, mentoring, and coaching with purpose. I believe in the power of routine, the impact of discipline, and the joy of helping others unlock their potential. Whether in the kitchen or the gym, I bring passion, precision, and a relentless commitment to growth.
